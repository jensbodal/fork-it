# Sloppy Joes

## Ingredients

* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 onion, diced
* 3/4 cup ketchup
* 2 tbsp vinegar
* 2 tbsp prepared mustard
* 1 tbsp sugar
* 1 tsp salt
* 0.5 tsp chili powder

## Directions

1. brown ground beef and onion in pan
1. mix in rest of ingredients and simmer
1. steam buns on top of mixture
1. serve :)
