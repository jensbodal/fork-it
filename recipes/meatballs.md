# Meatballs

**Tags:** Meatballs

## INGREDIENTS

* 1 lb **ground beef**
* 1/2 tsp **coriander**
* 1/2 tsp **majoram**
* 1/2 tsp **fennel seeds**, ground
* **Salt and Pepper**
* 1/2 tsp **chili flakes**
* 1/2 tsp **paprika**
* 2 slice **bacon**
* 1/2 cup **cauliflower rice**
* 1 tsp **fish sauce**
* 4 cloves **roasted garlic**
* 1 **egg**
* 1/4 **onion**, fine chopped
* **parmesean**, grated


## DIRECTIONS

Mix all ingredients together. Bake in oven or direclty in a marinara sauce.
